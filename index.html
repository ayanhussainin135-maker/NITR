About the Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering focuses on providing strong theoretical knowledge and practical skills in the fields of electronics, communication systems, and emerging technologies. The department aims to prepare students for industry, higher studies, and research through quality education and modern laboratory facilities.
